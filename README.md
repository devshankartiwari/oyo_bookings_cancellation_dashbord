# oyo_bookings_cancellation_dashbord
# 🏨 OYO Hotel Bookings Cancellation Analysis Dashboard

> 📊 A data-driven Power BI dashboard to analyze booking cancellations, identify key patterns, and uncover actionable business insights.


---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes hotel booking data to understand **cancellation behavior** across different dimensions such as:

- Lead time
- Hotel type
- Payment method
- Customer segments
- Booking channels

The goal is to help businesses **reduce cancellations, optimize revenue, and improve decision-making**.

---

## 🎯 Key Objectives

- 📉 Identify factors driving high cancellation rates  
- ⏳ Analyze impact of lead time on cancellations  
- 💳 Compare prepaid vs pay-at-hotel behavior  
- 🏨 Evaluate cancellation trends by hotel type  
- 📊 Provide actionable insights for business strategy  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- **Power BI** – Dashboard creation & visualization  
- **Excel / CSV** – Data source  
- **DAX** – Measures & calculated fields  
- **Data Cleaning** – Preprocessing & transformation  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

### 🔹 Key Metrics
- **Total Bookings:** ~1000+
- **Cancellation Rate:** ~83%
- **Average Lead Time:** ~30 days
- **Pay at Hotel %:** ~50%

---

### 📈 Insights

#### 1. Lead Time vs Cancellation
- Longer lead times → **higher cancellation probability**
- Peak cancellations occur in **22–30 days range**

#### 2. Payment Type Impact
- **Pay-at-hotel bookings have higher cancellation rates**
- Prepaid bookings are more reliable

#### 3. Hotel Type Analysis
- Budget/City hotels show **higher cancellations**
- Resort bookings tend to be more stable

#### 4. Cancellation Reasons
Top drivers:
- Payment failure  
- Price issues  
- Change of plans  
- No show  

--

## 🧠 Business Recommendations

- ✅ Encourage **prepaid bookings** via discounts  
- ⏳ Apply stricter policies for **long lead-time bookings**  
- 💡 Improve payment systems to reduce failures  
- 🎯 Target high-risk customers with retention strategies
